OFF TO BOSTON

Eager not to play in Lebron James shadow anymore. Kyrie Irving asked to be traded at the end of the 2016-2017 season. He is sent to Boston in exchange of Isaiah Thomas, Jae Crowder, Ante Žižić and a 2018 1st round draft pick.
Despite a hard season debut due to Gordon Hayward very bad injury. The Boston Celtics go on a 16 game winning streak. Especially against the defending champions, the Golden State Warriors. Kyrie Irving will serve as the Celtics point guard for two years before signing a 4 years deal with the Nets in 2019.
